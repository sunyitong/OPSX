# Safety Notes

OPSX is an experimental open-hardware project for camera repair, research, and modification.

## Mechanical Risk

The Polaroid SX-70 contains delicate mechanical assemblies. Disassembly and board replacement require care, patience, and appropriate tools.

Read the SX-70 repair reference before disassembly:

<https://instantphotography.files.wordpress.com/2010/12/polaroid-sx-70-camera-repair-book.pdf>

## Electrical Risk

- Inspect solder joints before applying power.
- Check for shorts on power rails.
- Do not actuate solenoids or motors without calibration.
- Watch for overheating during early tests.
- Disconnect power before reworking the board.

## Firmware Risk

Incorrect shutter or motor timings can damage mechanical parts. Calibrate gradually and document working values for each camera body.
